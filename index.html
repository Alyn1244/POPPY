POPPY is one of the main characters in the Trolls movie series. She's the upbeat, optimistic, and relentlessly cheerful princess of the Trolls, a group of colorful, singing and dancing creatures.She's a central figure in the Trolls movies and is always on a mission to spread happiness and protect her fellow Trolls from any threats.Poppy is the ever-positive leader of the troll community. She is always ready for a party complete with glitter,hugs and new friends. But don't confuse her cherry good nature and friendly personality for weakness.

BRANCH is the over-cautious paranoid survivalist in Troll Village, who lives in fear of invasion from the Bergens. The only Troll in the village who doesn't sing, dance or hug, Branch lives a disgruntled existence, constanly trying to prepare for the worst.
